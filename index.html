Cinematography

Cinematography is the art and technique of capturing moving images on film or digital media. It encompasses the use of camera angles, lighting, composition, color, and movement to visually tell a story, evoke emotions, and create atmosphere within a film or video production. A skilled cinematographer crafts visual narratives that enhance the storytelling, immerse audiences in the world of the film, and bring the director's vision to life on screen.
